From the Lizard Cove:

In our ongoing pursuit to encourage, document, and connect creative expression across Canada, we cut through waves upon waves of ethereal sounds, wyld shreddery, and brooding drones. It is magnificent. Some of the best grips escape our grasping fists. We list them here: birdboy : POLYBIUS I (Self Released :: Toronto, Ontario) bass birdie : Pink Dream (Self Released :: toronto, Ontario) Sheik : Go Away From Yourself (Call Out Records :: Saint John, New Brunswick) Bitter Fictions : Jettison (Shaking Box :: Calgary, Alberta) Joy Scouts : Jet Lag (Absurd Collective :: Edmonton and Montreal, Alberta) Charles Barabé : Cicatrices (Never Anything Records :: Victoriaville, Québec) Wreckage With Stick : To The Delight Of No One (Misery Loves Co Tapes :: Montreal, Québec) Untrained Animals : Obsolescent the Moment You Get It (self-released :: Calgary, Alberta) Colin Fisher : Secret Harmonies (HAVNrecords :: Hamilton, Ontario) Best Fern : Best Fern EP (Self Released :: Montreal, Québec) tinycastles : vines (Self Released :: Montreal, Québec) Potion : Marlee Moonchild's Space Dementia (rice toys :: montreal, Québec)

From the Lizard Cove:

Notre quête constante visant à encourager, à mettre en contact et à décrire les pratiques d’expression créatrice au Canada nous fait traverser vague après vague de sons éthérés, de shreds déchaînés et de sinistres bourdonnements; et c’est magnifique. De véritables joyaux échappent parfois à nos mains avides. Ils sont énumérés ici : birdboy : POLYBIUS I (Self Released :: Toronto, Ontario) bass birdie : Pink Dream (Self Released :: toronto, Ontario) Sheik : Go Away From Yourself (Call Out Records :: Saint John, New Brunswick) Bitter Fictions : Jettison (Shaking Box :: Calgary, Alberta) Joy Scouts : Jet Lag (Absurd Collective :: Edmonton and Montreal, Alberta) Charles Barabé : Cicatrices (Never Anything Records :: Victoriaville, Québec) Wreckage With Stick : To The Delight Of No One (Misery Loves Co Tapes :: Montreal, Québec) Untrained Animals : Obsolescent the Moment You Get It (self-released :: Calgary, Alberta) Colin Fisher : Secret Harmonies (HAVNrecords :: Hamilton, Ontario) Best Fern : Best Fern EP (Self Released :: Montreal, Québec) tinycastles : vines (Self Released :: Montreal, Québec) Potion : Marlee Moonchild's Space Dementia (rice toys :: montreal, Québec)
