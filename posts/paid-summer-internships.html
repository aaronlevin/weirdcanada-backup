Translator:

The translator is responsible for translating content from English into French.

Skills:

  • Fully bilingual (French and English) with very strong translation skills.
  • Basic knowledge of spreadsheets, documents, search engine, and email are required.
  • Candidate must be self-motivated and self-disciplined.
  • Strong attention to detail and communication skills are assets.
  • We value do-it-yourself culture. Resourcefulness, a strong work ethic, and creativity are valued above experience.

Duties:

  • Translate website content from English to French (and occasionally French to English).
  • Implement and maintain markup consistency for translated copy.
  • Coordinate outreach to Francophone artists, translators, and French-speaking communities.

Mandatory

  • Located in Kitchener or Ottawa for 8 weeks this summer.
  • Age 30 or under at the start of the job.
  • Was a full-time student in the last academic year, and is considering doing more school.
  • Is a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or refugee, and an Ontario resident.

Details

  • 8 weeks of work: June 20-August 15, 2016 (with some minor flexibility)
  • Kitchener, Ontario or Ottawa, Ontario.
  • Compensated at $11.25/hour
  • 30-35 hours per week
