From the named nuances of Aaron Levin:
After several rounds of consumer testing, we've discovered that WXWC: Wyrd X Weird Canada is a considerably better name for our Toronto micro-fest than NXN Wyrd. So, we changed it. Thankfully we haven't changed the fantastic lineup, beautiful venues, or bounty of surprise. We did, however, keep the digital home for purchasing tickets the same to ease your conscience mind (and wallet). We're also proud to unleash the poster designed and screen-printed by Andrew Zukerman which will be appearing in select Toronto record stores and hang-outs very soon.
