From the imminent wyrdness of Gabriel Jasmin and Aaron Levin:
In preparation for our Montreal invasion, we asked all the Wyrd MTL II artists to submit a short introduction about them and their upcoming performance at the festival. The video gives a sneak peek into the imminent wyrdness waiting to take over Casa del Popolo and Sala Rossa this Thursday June 14. In addition to the video, Montreal's preeminent purveyour of experimental synthesis, Hobo Cubes, has put together a mix of sounds from artists playing Wyrd MTL II. You can catch Hobo Cubes DJ'ing alongside Aaron Levin outside the Casa stage. We can't wait to see all of you in just a few days!
